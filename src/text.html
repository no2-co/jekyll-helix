Sales Tax

Sales tax is determined by the shipping address entered at checkout, and at present only applies to residents of new york. sales of clothing and shoes totaling less than $110 will not incur sales tax; sales totaling more than $110, as well as all accessories, home goods, etc. will incur 8.875% sales tax. sales tax will not apply to the purchase of gift certificates; however, sales tax may be charged when the gift certificate is redeemed.
